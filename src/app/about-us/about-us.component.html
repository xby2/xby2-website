A Different Approach

Why does the world need another IT consulting firm? It doesn't, at least not the typical IT consulting firm. Firms over promising and under delivering when it comes to quality and results. Firms that use their connections rather than client outcomes to generate future sales. Firms that create a cycle of dependence with their clients to generate continued revenue for themselves. Firms with "strategic partnerships" that undermine objectivity and taint consulting analyses with bias. Firms where successful delivery is achieved only sometimes, and accountability for results is rare to find. Firms where the "A" team makes the sale and the "B" team is brought in to deliver.

A different approach was needed. With this premise, X by 2 was founded in 1998 on a different set of principles to do what is lacking in typical IT consulting firms.

  • Independence and Objectivity - advocate solely for our client’s interests. We are open and transparent about any conflicts of interests. We have no hidden agendas or products to sell or advocate for.
  • Impact and Results Driven - deliver strategic value and results. We believe having impact and results will speak for itself when it comes to growing and sustaining our business.
  • Quality over Quantity - increase impact and reduce total cost of ownership of solutions through the quality of people we hire and retain. We hire exceptional people, so we can deliver exceptional solutions and results with the smallest number of people necessary to deliver them. There is no "B" team.
  • Client Partnership and Self-Sufficiency - enhance client capability by working side-by-side with our clients and freely sharing our knowledge and experience. We're here to help solve difficult and challenging problems with our clients, but we also want to help our clients take control of their own future.

In the end, it turns out that a different approach is exactly what the industry desired, creating the opportunity for X by 2 to serve some of the most forward thinking insurance and healthcare organizations in North America over the last 20 years.

Our day-to-day architecture practice is different than what it was in 1998, and it will be different in the future for certain. That’s business and technology for you. But our core principles and beliefs have not changed and will continue to define who we are as a company and how we approach serving our clients.
