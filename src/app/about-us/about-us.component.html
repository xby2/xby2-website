A Different Approach

Why does the world need another IT consulting firm? It doesn't, at least not the typical IT consulting firm. Firms over promising and under delivering when it comes to quality and results. Firms that use their connections rather than client outcomes to generate future sales. Firms that create a cycle of dependence with their clients to generate continued revenue for themselves. Firms with "strategic partnerships" that undermine objectivity and taint consulting analyses with bias. Firms where successful delivery is achieved only sometimes, and accountability for results is rare to find. Firms where the "A" team makes the sale and the "B" team is brought in to deliver.

A different approach was needed. With this premise, X by 2 was founded in 1998 on a different set of principles to do what is lacking in typical IT consulting firms.
