Single Page Application Architecture - A Project Proven Approach

Presented by:

Jeff Sallans, Associate Director

Jason Brown, Senior Consultant

Single Page Applications (SPA) are designed to provide customers with a seamless user experience without the delay of reloading page content. After attending this webinar you'll have a better understanding of Single Page Application Architecture and the business value it provides. We'll cover common architecture patterns, the hiring and training process, and common pitfalls when working in this domain.
